About Me

One day, while talking with someone I deeply respect, they said they wished more people would visit this island. That was when I realised there were no local tour guides here.

I decided to study English and lived in Australia for two years. Through those experiences, I learned about many cultures — and also rediscovered the beauty of rural Japan.

In October 2025, I returned home and started Inaka Tours to share the quiet charm of countryside life with visitors.
